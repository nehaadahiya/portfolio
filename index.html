Hello, It's Me

And I'm a Data Analyst

With a foundation in Computer Science Engineering and a deep love for storytelling, I transform raw data into insights that inspire action. I thrive at the intersection of logic and intuition.
My toolkit includes Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau and machine learning — but my real superpower lies in asking the right questions and turning data noise into clear, strategic symphonies.
Whether decoding Netflix content trends or analyzing stock market pulses, I believe in insights that don't just inform, but empower.
When I’m not wrangling data, you'll find me lost in poetry, designing visuals, or exploring stories hidden between the lines of everyday life.
Let's turn complex data into your next big decision.
